Welcome

Parametric Concepts explores music as parametric properties of sound, instrument and performer. Parametric distinction and interactivity defines the compositional structure and enables expressions beyond traditional musical conceptual definitions.

Compositional ideas are structured around parametric principles related to the inherent properties and variables of the instrument, the sound, and the performer. The musical content is not necessarily defined as melody, rhythm or timbre (altough at times the heard result may be similar!), but rather as interactive functions of the situational variables.
