Welcome

Parametric Concepts explores music as parametric properties of sound, instrument and performer. Parametric distinction and interactivity defines the compositional structure and enables expressions beyond traditional musical conceptual definitions.

Concepts = ideas/compositions/expressions structured around specific parameters related to the inherent properties of instrument, sound, and/or performer. Instead of relying on the conventional constructs of melody, rhythm, or timbre, the musical content acts as a direct response to situational variables (even if the result may sound similar at times).

Knobs, strings, volume, wood, hesitation, pitch, friction, feet, tradition, switches, silence, keys, memory, error, cables, duration, posture, size, gaze, housing, expectation, color, resonance, fingers, decay, sight, room, intention, dampening, habit, mass, listening, distance, contour, breath, acoustics, failure, density, reflex, space, proximity, weight, gesture, phase, resistance, intuition, timing, architecture, sweat, threshold, texture, focus, velocity, release.
