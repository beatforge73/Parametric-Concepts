Parametric Concepts 1

2026

Exploring parametric concepts using an analog synthesizer. Mapping the space and interplay between sound parameters and musical expression.

Each track is a sonic exploration of the instruments inherent properties, making for unique musical experiences that challenge traditional notions of melody, rhythm, and timbre. From evolving textures to dynamic interactions. Sound shaped by the parameters that define it.

The music is composed/improvised/performed live on a Moog Matriarch synthesizer. Recorded in stereo directly to a DAW. No post-processing or editing. Released as a collection of 24-bit/48kHz .wav files, available for streaming and download.

Tracklist

01. Not Chords
Filtering, levels, delay feedback ++. The pitches form the background, the rest of the instrument is the composition. Melody is timbre and movement. Realize eventually, you are not hearing chords.
02. a matter of available voices
Interplay of quasi-duality. Harmonics interacting thru gain staging, modulation and keyboard allocation. Notes are relevant, but not defining. Texture comes from electrons, did you know that?
03. Borderline groovy
Fixed pulse, normal music. Could probably dance to this.
04. Triangles intro
No comment
05. Triangles
Only triangles. 5 of them. Amazing what triangles can achieve. Mixer is crucial, and playing around with feedback modulations.
06. RND?
Fascinating how different instruments approach randomness differently. What feels unpredictable one moment might not the next; eventually, the chaotic just becomes the new normal.
07. Suppressing feedback
Chasing beautiful sounds. How long do I let i ring before I act? This is mind-body interaction, I am the soft clipper, the ceiling is me.
08. Voice allocation improvisation
Simply exploring how the instrument handles multiple keyboard notes. So many variables interacting, feels much more complex than it sounds.
