Parametric Concepts 1

2026

Exploring parametric concepts on analog synthesizers, this album delves into the interplay of sound parameters and musical expression. Each track is a sonic exploration of the inherent properties of the instrument, creating a unique auditory experience that challenges traditional notions of melody, rhythm, and timbre. From evolving textures to dynamic interactions, "Parametric Concepts 1" invites listeners to immerse themselves in a world where sound is shaped by the parameters that define it.
Project methodology: Music is composed, improvised and performed live on a Moog Matriarch synthesizer. Recorder in stereo directly to a DAW, no post-processing or editing other than basic level adjustments. The album is released as a collection of 24-bit/48kHz .wav files, and also available in compressed .mp3 format for streaming and download.
